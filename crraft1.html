CRRAFT Robotics

Enhancing Childhood Robotics Education

Our Mission Our Vision
Our Mission: The CRRAFT Partnership is dedicated to promoting computational thinking in early childhood through culturally relevant robotics programs. By collaborating with educators, families, and communities, we aim to create engaging learning experiences that connect children’s cultural backgrounds with computer science, fostering a lifelong interest in technology and helping to diversify the future workforce.
Our Vision: To cultivate a diverse and inclusive future in computer science by empowering young children, particularly those from underrepresented communities, with the skills and sense of belonging necessary to thrive in a technologically-driven world.
